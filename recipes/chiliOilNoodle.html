Chili Oil Noodle Recipe

Description

Spicy Szechuan noodles in chili oil are a fiery and flavorful dish from China. Cooked noodles are tossed in a spicy chili oil infused with Szechuan peppercorns and other spices. The noodles are glossy and vibrant, with a satisfying chewiness. They are often topped with vegetables and proteins for added texture. The dish is known for its intense heat and bold flavors, making it a favorite for spice lovers.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Soy Sauce
  • Black Vinegar
  • Oil
  • Chili pepper flake
  • Garlic
  • Sesame seed
  • Sugar and Salt
  • Cilantro and Green Onion

Steps

  1. Heat the oil over medium low heat. Once heated, saut√© the garlic until it becomes fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Turn off the heat and while the garlic oil is still hot, add in the Szechuan pepper flakes, soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and sugar. Stir to combine.
  3. Boil the noodles according to the directions and rinse under cold water. Drain well.
  4. Add scallions, cilantro, sesame seeds, and garlic chili oil sauce to your liking. Toss to combine and enjoy!
